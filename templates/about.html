About Certificate Manager

Professional certificate management for events, education, and more.

Certificate Manager is a professional certificate management system designed to streamline the process of organizing events, managing participants, and generating certificates.

Our Mission

To provide educational institutions, organizations, and event organizers with a simple, efficient, and professional tool for managing their certification processes.

Key Features

  • Event Management
  • Student Database
  • Excel Integration
  • Custom Branding
  • PDF Generation
  • Email Distribution
  • Multi-Event Support
  • Professional Templates

Technology Stack

Built with modern web technologies including Flask (Python), Bootstrap 5, SQLAlchemy, and ReportLab for PDF generation.
